What I Changed & Why
A side-by-side look at the original messaging from your current site and the refreshed copy — with the reasoning behind each change.
OriginalIssueRefreshed
"Jesus Loves You, And So Do We!"Generic, expected, low emotional impact"You Belong Here Before You Feel Like You Do."
"Imperfect people seeking after a perfect Savior"Great idea — buried in body copySurfaced as a primary brand statement throughout the site
"Just Come As You Are"Works — keeping itKept as-is. Simple, warm, effective.
"How Can We Help?" (repeated everywhere)Repetitive, generic, no urgencyVaried: "Reach Out," "Let's Talk," "We're Here"
Give page — nearly emptyMassive missed opportunity for generosity cultureFull page: philosophy, 3 options, impact cards, scripture, embed
Beliefs section in dense paragraphsHard to read, overwhelming for new visitorsScannable cards with short summaries + scripture references
"OUR MISSION" as a section labelWeak header — no pull, no personality"Leading. Loving. Living." — pillars become the headline
